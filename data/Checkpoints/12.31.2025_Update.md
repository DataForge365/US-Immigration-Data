## Data Model Summary

**6 Tables defined in data_dictionary.md:**

| Table | Purpose | Primary Source |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| `arrivals` | People entering US at ports | CBP, I-94, DHS Yearbook |
| `visa_issuances` | Visas issued by embassies | State Department |
| `visa_refusals` | Denied visa applications | State Department |
| `inadmissibles` | Denied entry at ports | CBP |
| `overstays` | Stayed past authorization | DHS Entry/Exit Reports |
| `sources` | Metadata tracking | Internal |

**Key columns standardized:**
- Dates: YYYY-MM format
- Countries: ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes (IND, CHN, MEX, etc.)
- Visa classes: Standardized codes (H-1B, F-1, B-1/B-2, etc.)
